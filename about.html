About

Astana IT University currently has one large dining room located on the first floor, but soon university wants to open another 5-6 cafeterias.

The University management sincerely wishes to provide students with high-quality hot meals, soft drinks and surprise them with the quality of products and services. This confirms the high level and the fact that university canteens are willingly visited not only by students, but also by teachers.
